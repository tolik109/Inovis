We inspire data to work by planning and developing creative and customizable software solutions and software products.

We create Web apps, Win Apps, Mobile apps, Big data solutions, data pipelines, data layers and reporting solutions. Our uniqueness is rooted in our experience to build operational software solutions coupled with managerial solutions to help your business grow and increase your profits.

Outsourcing/Outstaffing

We are software experts with many years of experience developing enterprise grade software solutions for our customers. Our efforts and experience have gained us the knowledge to build teams for projects and recruit experts for specific tasks. We apply our experience and knowledge to recruit the most appropriate employees for each open position.

Data services

We have developed for our customers many types of data solutions from big data pipelines used to ship data from sources to a central data lake, through data crunching to process data and transform it to its final format and store the data in a data layer. We develop reporting layers above the data layer to grant access to the data.

Software Solution Development

We offer our customers a wide range of data solutions: Web Apps, Mobile Apps, Windows Apps, Data pipelines, Data Layers and Reporting Layers. We develop solutions based on open source technologies or commercial licensing by Microsoft. The solutions we develop from drawing board to final product can be planned to be deployed on the cloud or on prem.

Products

Our advantage is in the experience we have gained building managerial software solutions because we see the product in a holistic view as a business and consider during our planning and design the future managerial business needs. We offer our services as a supplier or as partners/investors.
